Krissy Cela

My journey into the fitness world was never about the perfect body, it was about making a positive change to feel better mentally and physically. Fitness made me feel stronger not only in my physique, but in my mind too. I want to share my new found confidence, and empower women around the world!

I am passionate to encourage women across the world to build inner and outer strength, form consistent habits and be the very best versions of themselves.

“If you want something you’ve never had, you must be willing to do something you’ve never done.” —Thomas Jefferson

As your trainer I am here to give you all the tools you need to become the very best version of yourself. Regardless of what your goals are, I am here to support and empower you. Remember you are never alone in this journey, we all are in this together and you are doing this for you!
